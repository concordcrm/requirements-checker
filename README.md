# Concord CRM requirements checker

You can check **most of the requirements** directly on your server, use our requirements checker to determine whether your server/hosting is compatible with [Concord CRM](https://www.concordcrm.com).

* Click the green button with text **Code** located at the top right side.
* Choose **Download ZIP**
* On your server, create a subdomain e.q. **crm.domain.com** with document root pointing to the `public` directory. [Read More](https://www.concordcrm.com/docs/installation#create-subdomain)
* Upload the files from the **.zip** file to the subdomain root e.q. `/user/public_html/crm.domain.com/public`
* Visit your subdomain URL.
